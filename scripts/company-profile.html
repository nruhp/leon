About Us

Our Legacy in Ceramics

Combining the rich heritage of Khurja pottery with modern manufacturing excellence.

Our Story

Established in **2017**, S.K. Industries began with a simple vision: to bring the world-renowned ceramic craftsmanship of Khurja to the global market with a focus on quality and consistency suited for modern hospitality needs.

What started as a modest unit has grown into a fully integrated facility. We take pride in being a leading manufacturer of **Stoneware**, while simultaneously serving as a premier marketer and packager of **Fine Bone China and Porcelain**, ensuring quality checks and premium packaging for all our clients.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be India's most trusted partner for premium ceramic tableware, recognized globally for innovation and durability.

Our Mission

To blend traditional artisanship with automated precision, delivering cost-effective, high-quality, and lead-free tableware solutions.
